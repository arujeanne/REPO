AIESEC

AIESEC is an international non-profit youth organization, and it was an honor for me to be a part of this great community. It is a great place for professional development, and there is a great corporate culture. During my terms in the positions of Marketing Department team member, Conference Committee President and Project Manager, I learned lots of lessons on how to lead a team, to organize a workflow of the project and how to make long-term strategic planning. It is a place where you can develop your leadership potential!

Sample Mag

SAMPLE Mag is the first ever fashion magazine in Nazarbayev University. It is a community of people, who are not indifferent towards the world of fashion and who want to highlight the significance of fashion in the world economy, as well as our everyday lives. We write articles, we make conceptual photoshoots, we design our magazine layouts, but also we assist in the photo/videoshoots organized by other clubs, and recently we conducted our first ever upcycling contest, followed by a fashion show.

NU Awards

NU Student Awards Committee is organized to appreciate the activities and achievements of the student clubs, organizations and the students. It recognizes and rewards those who demonstrated their talents and desire to diversify the social life of our community.
